Redundancy Ray

Redundancy Ray helps people estimate their redundancy payout and understand how far it might go. It can show a simple breakdown of payments and give a rough idea of how long a payout could support mortgage repayments.

BabyPay

BabyPay is for parents planning time off work. It brings together government parental leave, employer-paid leave, and estimated after-tax income, so families can see what their income might look like over the months around a new baby.

Simple Flow turns a list of steps into a flowchart automatically. As you write out the process, the chart is drawn for you, making it easier to explain and share how things work.
